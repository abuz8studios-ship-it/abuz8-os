# Carousel Formula

Standard 10-slide carousel used for X posts in this skill.

Slide 1: Hook — bold statement or question
Slide 2: Problem statement — who has this pain?
Slide 3: Why it matters — cost of inaction
Slide 4: Core concept or mental model
Slide 5: Step 1 of the fix
Slide 6: Step 2
Slide 7: Step 3 (if needed)
Slide 8: Real proof — screenshot, metric, or anecdote
Slide 9: TL;DR — one-sentence recap
Slide 10: CTA — abuz8ai.com + action verb
